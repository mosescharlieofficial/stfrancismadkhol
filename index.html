Welcome to Educational Premium

St. Francis De Sales English Medium School

Managed beautifully by the Pune Fransalian Education Society, our institution has stood as a beacon of complete, values‑based quality learning in Madkhol‑Dhavadki.

Founded with a progressive vision to deliver premium modern standard metrics of learning to local and expanding communities, we offer a dynamic, child‑centered environment from early foundational steps through advanced middle‑school learning tracks.

Medium of Instruction

Strictly English Medium, maintaining highly articulated speech standards alongside comprehensive cultural studies.

Our Vision

A holistic society of compassionately human, spiritually enlightened, intellectually awakened, professionally skilled, socially committed, culturally integrated persons based on the values as lived and taught by Jesus Christ and followed by Saint Francis De Sales.

Our Mission

To provide a conducive atmosphere for intellectual development. To facilitate conviction-based spiritual formation. To nurture a sense of the divine and Transcendence. To impart the virtues of gentleness, compassion and optimism. To impart an authentic leadership training. To instill wholesome professional ethics. To encourage sports, games and work experience. To inculcate healthy food habits. To promote environmental consciousness. To extend an integral education to all. To foster a caring and human society meant to promote the marginalized.

"Education is not merely preparing structural processing tools for life; it is life itself unfolding dynamically."

Dear Parents, Visitors, and Students, welcome to our vibrant space of growth. Here at St. Francis De Sales, our professional mandate balances rigid logical framework skillsets with moral excellence.
